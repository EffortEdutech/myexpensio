📋 Integration Notes

  • 🔄 Replace {{inviter_name}} dynamically with the name of the admin or user who sent the invite.
  • 🗓️ Set deletion period consistently — 14 days is a safe default. Make sure your system actually deletes unused invite emails after this window.
  • 🔗 Update the Terms and Privacy links to your live URLs before launch.
  • 🏢 Fill in the company legal name and registered address before go-live — required under PDPA.
  • 📩 Use a noreply@ sender but always provide a reply-to or contact email for deletion requests.
  • 🗄️ Log invite sends server-side: invitee_email, invited_at, invited_by, expires_at, legal_footer_version.
  • 🌏 If you send to non-Malaysians in future, this footer may need localisation for GDPR or other frameworks.
