📋 Integration Notes

  • Store consent separately: consent_terms (required) and consent_marketing (optional).
  • Record consented_at timestamp and consented_ip for each user at signup.
  • The Create account button must remain disabled until consent_terms is checked.
  • Do not pre-check either checkbox — PDPA requires affirmative, informed consent.
  • Do not bundle marketing consent with terms consent — separate checkboxes required.
  • Link to the live URLs of your privacy-policy.html and terms-of-service.html.
